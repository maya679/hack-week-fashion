Welcome to STYLEX!

Here at STYLEX you can exchange your gently used clothes with others to spice up your wardrobe. Click "Add a Gem" to upload a photo and description of some old treasures you are willing to part with. Then, peruse what others have to offer on the "Browse" page. STYLEX is as easy as that. Start collecting gems now!

STYLEX was created by two Girls Who Code alumni in the summer of 2015 at the Electronic Arts location. Maya Miller and Hayley Howell are pictured below.
